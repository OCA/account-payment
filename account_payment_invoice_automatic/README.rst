Automatically charge the Credit Card for Invoices with a stored Payment Token.

**Table of contents**

.. contents::
   :local:

Usage
=====

- On a draft Invoice, set the Payment Token, in the Other Info tab
- Confirm the Invoice
- The credit card payment will automatically be processed
